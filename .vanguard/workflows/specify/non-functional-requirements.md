# Step 5/6: Non-Functional Requirements

> Identify performance, security, and quality requirements

## Objective

Identify performance, security, quality requirements

## Inputs Required

- Functional requirements

## Outputs Produced

- Non-functional requirements with measurable criteria

---

Define non-functional requirements:
- Performance (response time, throughput)
- Security (authentication, authorization, data protection)
- Reliability (uptime, error handling)
- Scalability (concurrent users, data volume)
- Accessibility (WCAG level)

---

## Checkpoint

Before proceeding to the next step, confirm:

> **Are quality attributes measurable?**

## Tips

- Make each requirement measurable
