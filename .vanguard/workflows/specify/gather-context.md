# Step 1/6: Gather Context

> Understand feature scope, target users, and business value

## Objective

Understand feature scope, target users, business value

## Inputs Required

- Feature request or idea

## Outputs Produced

- Feature summary
- Target users
- Business value statement

---

Start by asking the user about the feature they want to build. Understand:
- What problem does it solve?
- Who are the target users?
- What business value does it provide?
- Are there existing solutions or constraints?

---

## Checkpoint

Before proceeding to the next step, confirm:

> **Do I understand what we are building and why?**

## Tips

- Ask open-ended questions first
- Look for existing discovery documents
