# Step 2/7: Define Experience Principles

> Define 3-5 experience principles that guide all design decisions

## Objective

Define guiding experience principles

## Inputs Required

- Emotional needs map

## Outputs Produced

- 3-5 experience principles

---

Collaboratively define 3-5 experience principles:
- Each principle should be actionable
- Include examples of what it means in practice
- Principles should resolve design conflicts

---

## Checkpoint

Before proceeding to the next step, confirm:

> **Do principles capture the essence of the desired experience?**

## Tips

- Good principles make tough decisions easier
