# Step 6/7: Accessibility Audit

> Ensure WCAG 2.1 AA compliance by design

## Objective

Ensure accessibility compliance from the start

## Inputs Required

- Component strategy
- User journeys

## Outputs Produced

- Accessibility requirements
- ARIA patterns needed

---

Audit design for accessibility:
- Keyboard navigation for all interactions
- Screen reader compatibility
- Color contrast ratios (4.5:1 minimum)
- Focus management
- Error identification and recovery

---

## Checkpoint

Before proceeding to the next step, confirm:

> **Does every interaction meet WCAG 2.1 AA?**

## Tips

- Test with keyboard-only navigation mentally
