# Step 3/4: Identify Risks

> Uncover potential problems before they become blockers

## Objective

Uncover potential problems before they become blockers

## Inputs Required

- Landscape map

## Outputs Produced

- Risk register
- Constraints list
- Assumptions documented

---

Identify risks, constraints, and assumptions:
- Technical risks (complexity, unknowns, dependencies)
- Resource risks (skills, timeline)
- Quality risks (tech debt, test coverage)
- Assumptions that need validation

---

## Checkpoint

Before proceeding to the next step, confirm:

> **Have I surfaced key risks and constraints?**

## Tips

- Rate risks by likelihood and impact
