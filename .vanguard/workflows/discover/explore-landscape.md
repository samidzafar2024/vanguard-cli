# Step 2/4: Explore Landscape

> Build a mental map of the territory being explored

## Objective

Build mental map of territory being explored

## Inputs Required

- Discovery scope

## Outputs Produced

- Landscape overview
- Key components identified

---

Explore the codebase or problem space:
- For brownfield: Read key files, map architecture, identify patterns
- For greenfield: Research similar solutions, identify technologies
- Map major components and their relationships

---

## Checkpoint

Before proceeding to the next step, confirm:

> **Do I have a high-level map of the territory?**

## Tips

- Start broad, then dive deep into interesting areas
- Look for documentation and tests first
