# Step 1/4: Understand Context

> Understand scope and goals of the discovery session

## Objective

Understand scope and goals of discovery session

## Inputs Required

- Project description or feature area

## Outputs Produced

- Discovery scope definition
- Key questions to answer

---

Start by understanding what needs to be discovered:
- Is this a brownfield (existing codebase) or greenfield project?
- What specific areas need exploration?
- What decisions depend on this discovery?

---

Before proceeding to the next step, confirm:
